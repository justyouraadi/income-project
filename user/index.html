Frequently Asked Questions

💰 Investment & Returns

What is the minimum investment amount? +

The minimum investment amount is $20. You can invest any amount above this threshold to start earning daily ROI and other income benefits.

How is Daily ROI calculated? +

Daily ROI is calculated based on your investment slab:
• $20 - $499: 0.5% daily
• $500 - $2,499: 0.6% daily
• $2,500 - $4,999: 0.7% daily
• $5,000 - $9,999: 0.8% daily
• $10,000+: 1.0% daily
ROI is credited to your wallet daily for 100 days.

What is the investment validity period? +

Each investment is valid for 100 days from the date of investment. You'll receive daily ROI credits throughout this period. After 100 days, you can make a new investment to continue earning.

👥 Referral & Team

How do I refer someone? +

Go to your Dashboard and copy your unique referral link. Share this link with friends and family. When they sign up using your link and make an investment, you'll earn 5% direct referral income on their investment amount.

What is Direct Income? +

Direct Income is 5% of every investment made by users you directly refer. For example, if your referral invests $1000, you earn $50 instantly. Note: You must be an active user (have made at least one investment) to receive referral bonuses.

What are the different income types? +

5 Income Types:
1. Daily ROI - Daily returns on your investment (0.5-1%)
2. Direct Income - 5% on direct referrals' investments
3. Slab Income - Level-based team investment bonus
4. Royalty Income - Based on company performance
5. Salary Income - Monthly salary based on team business

💸 Withdrawals & Transfers

How do I withdraw funds? +

Go to the Withdraw section, enter the amount you wish to withdraw, and provide your UPI ID or bank details. Minimum withdrawal is $10. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours after admin approval.

What is P2P Transfer? +

P2P (Peer-to-Peer) Transfer allows you to send funds from your main wallet to another user's account using their User ID or email. An OTP verification is required for security. Transfer fees may apply.

Why is my withdrawal pending? +

All withdrawals go through an admin review process for security. Typical approval time is 24-48 hours. If your withdrawal is pending for longer, please contact support with your withdrawal request details.

🔐 Account & Security

How do I change my password? +

Currently, password changes must be done through support. Please contact our support team via email or WhatsApp with your registered email address to request a password reset.

What does "Inactive" status mean? +

New accounts start with "Inactive" status. Your account becomes "Active" once you make your first investment. Active status is required to receive referral bonuses from your team.

Is my investment safe? +

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted data transmission, secure payment processing, and regular security audits. However, as with any investment, please invest responsibly and only what you can afford.
